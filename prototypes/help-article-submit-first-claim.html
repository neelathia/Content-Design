How to Submit Your First Claim

📋 Overview

Submitting your first claim in the Enterprise Portal is straightforward once you understand the process. This guide will walk you through every step, from preparing your documentation to tracking your claim's progress.

A claim is a request for rebate payment based on your sales activities during a specific period. Claims typically include volume rebates, performance bonuses, or special promotional incentives.

💡
Pro Tip: Before starting your first claim, take a moment to review your contract terms and rebate structure. This will help you understand what you're eligible to claim.

🎯 Before You Start

Make sure you have the following information ready:

  • Sales data for the claim period (invoices, purchase orders)
  • Contract reference numbers and applicable rebate rates
  • Supporting documentation (delivery receipts, customer confirmations)
  • End-customer information (if applicable to your agreement)

Required File Formats

The portal accepts the following file formats for documentation:

  • PDF files (preferred for invoices and contracts)
  • Excel spreadsheets (.xlsx, .xls)
  • Images (.jpg, .png) for receipts or photos
  • CSV files for bulk data uploads
⚠️
Important: Claims must be submitted within 90 days of the end of the claim period. Late submissions may not be processed.

🚀 Step-by-Step Instructions

1
Navigate to Claims Section

From the main portal dashboard, click on "Claims" in the top navigation menu. This will take you to your claims overview page.

[Screenshot: Claims navigation menu highlighted]
2
Start New Claim

Click the "Upload Claim" button in the top-right corner of the claims page. This will open the claim submission form.

3
Fill in Basic Information

Complete the following required fields:

  • Claim Period: Select the month/quarter you're claiming for
  • Location: Choose your business location
  • Contract Reference: Select the applicable agreement
  • Claim Type: Choose volume rebate, performance bonus, etc.
4
Add Line Items

For each product or service you're claiming:

  • Enter the SKU/Product Code
  • Specify the Quantity sold
  • Input the Unit Price
  • Select the End Customer (if required)

The system will automatically calculate rebate amounts based on your contract terms.

5
Upload Supporting Documents

Attach relevant documentation to support your claim:

  • Click "Add Document"
  • Select files from your computer
  • Add a brief description for each document
  • Ensure all files are under 10MB each
6
Review and Submit

Before submitting:

  • Review all line items for accuracy
  • Verify calculated amounts match your expectations
  • Ensure all required documents are attached
  • Add any additional comments or notes

Click "Submit Claim" to send for processing.

✅ After Submission

Once you've submitted your claim:

  1. Confirmation: You'll receive a confirmation email with your claim reference number
  2. Initial Review: Our team will conduct an initial review within 2-3 business days
  3. Processing: Complete processing typically takes 5-10 business days
  4. Approval: Approved claims are processed for payment within 15 business days

Tracking Your Claim

You can track your claim's progress at any time:

  • Go to the Claims section of the portal
  • Find your claim in the list
  • Check the Status column for current progress
  • Click on the claim reference for detailed information

🔧 Troubleshooting

Common Issues and Solutions

❌ "Missing Required Field" Error

Ensure all mandatory fields are completed. Required fields are marked with a red asterisk (*).

❌ "File Upload Failed" Error

Check that your file is under 10MB and in an accepted format. Try refreshing the page and uploading again.

❌ "Invalid SKU" Warning

Verify the product code against your contract or price list. Use the SKU search feature to find the correct code.

❌ "Calculation Error" Message

Double-check quantities and prices. Ensure you're using the correct contract terms and rebate rates.
