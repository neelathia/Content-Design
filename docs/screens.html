This section documents the layout, content questions, and role-based variations across major screens in the commercial banking experience.

Dashboard

Roles:

  • Initiator: Shows task initiation, status of own submissions
  • Approver: Shows pending approvals with urgency indicators
  • Auditor: Shows system-level alerts and violations

Primary Content Questions:

  • What do I need to act on?
  • What is overdue or pending?
  • What did I submit recently?

Routing

Roles:

  • Initiator only: Assigns an approver and adds optional note

Content Questions:

  • Who is approving this?
  • Should I add context?

Review + Submit

Roles:

  • Initiator: Sees editable summary and a final submit button
  • Approver: Sees approve/reject options
  • Auditor: Read-only mode

Primary Questions:

  • Does this look correct?
  • What will happen after submission?

Confirmation

  • Message: "Your payment is ready to be reviewed"
  • Safeguard: "No changes will be made until approved"
  • Tracking: Link to status log or audit trail
