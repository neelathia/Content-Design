Role-Based Strategy

Purpose

This page defines how content and design flex based on user role – ensuring the experience is context-aware and streamlined for each persona.

Initiator (Analyst)

  • Focus: Initiating payments, viewing recent status updates, navigating process steps.
  • Tone: Clear, instructional, supportive.
  • Key Questions: What do I need to submit? What’s pending? Where do I go next?

Approver (Manager)

  • Focus: Reviewing submissions, approving or rejecting with ease.
  • Tone: Efficient, confident, urgency-aware.
  • Key Questions: What’s waiting for me? Are there any urgent items? What’s the risk?

Auditor (Compliance Officer)

  • Focus: Monitoring policy exceptions, generating reports, ensuring traceability.
  • Tone: Factual, neutral, formal.
  • Key Questions: What’s out of policy? Where are the logs? What requires action?
