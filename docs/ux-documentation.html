User Research & Analysis

Target User Roles

AP Analyst
Daily payment processing specialist
Key Responsibilities
  • Process daily payment workflows
  • Manage vendor relationships
  • Track payment status and history
  • Handle payment exceptions and errors
Pain Points
  • Time-consuming manual processes
  • Limited real-time status visibility
  • Difficulty prioritizing urgent payments
  • Complex approval workflows
Goals
  • Process payments quickly and accurately
  • Easily identify priority items
  • Access complete payment information
  • Manage workload efficiently
AP Manager
Team oversight and strategic planning
Key Responsibilities
  • Oversee team performance
  • Manage approval workflows
  • Monitor payment trends and metrics
  • Ensure compliance and accuracy
Pain Points
  • Limited visibility into team workload
  • Slow approval processes
  • Manual reporting requirements
  • Difficulty tracking performance metrics
Goals
  • Monitor team efficiency
  • Streamline approval processes
  • Access analytics and reporting
  • Optimize workflow performance
Compliance Officer
Regulatory oversight and risk management
Key Responsibilities
  • Ensure regulatory compliance
  • Monitor risk indicators
  • Prepare audit documentation
  • Review policy adherence
Pain Points
  • Manual compliance monitoring
  • Incomplete audit trails
  • Reactive risk identification
  • Time-intensive reporting
Goals
  • Proactive risk management
  • Efficient audit preparation
  • Automated compliance reporting
  • Real-time policy monitoring

Information Architecture

Site Structure

Truist Commercial Banking Platform ├── Role Selection Landing Page │ ├── AP Analyst Portal │ ├── AP Manager Portal │ └── Compliance Officer Portal │ ├── AP Analyst Section │ ├── Dashboard (Status + Quick Actions) │ ├── Payment List (Filterable) │ ├── Payment Details │ ├── Batch Operations │ └── Personal Settings │ ├── AP Manager Section │ ├── Dashboard (Team Metrics) │ ├── Approval Queue │ ├── Team Performance │ ├── Analytics & Reports │ └── Management Tools │ └── Compliance Section ├── Dashboard (Compliance Status) ├── Risk Assessment ├── Audit Trail Management ├── Regulatory Reports └── Policy Management

Content Prioritization Strategy

Above the Fold (Mobile Priority)
1. Critical status indicators - Immediate attention items
2. Primary action buttons - Most common user tasks
3. Urgent/important items - High-priority payments
4. Clear navigation - Role-specific menu structure
Below the Fold (Secondary)
1. Secondary actions - Less frequent operations
2. Historical data - Payment history and trends
3. Detailed information - Extended payment details
4. Settings and preferences - User customization options

Interaction Design Patterns

Progressive Disclosure

Level 1: Dashboard Overview
High-level status indicators and critical metrics providing immediate situational awareness
Level 2: List View
Key payment details with visual status indicators for quick scanning and prioritization
