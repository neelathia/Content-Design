My Research Approach

Understanding the varied needs and pain points of different commercial banking roles was foundational to this design. My process involved:

  • Initial Problem Definition: Identifying key inefficiencies in manual accounts payable processes
  • Persona Development: Creating detailed personas based on user roles to inform specific user journeys and content needs
  • Workflow Mapping: Analyzing existing workflows to identify critical touchpoints and opportunities for digital transformation
  • Competitive Analysis: Reviewed existing solutions and identified a critical gap in real-time payment tracking across major banking platforms

Design Decision Impact

Based on the 8-12% error rate for AP Analysts, my design prioritizes robust input validation and clear feedback mechanisms. The 6-8 minute approval time led me to focus on streamlined approval workflows with contextual information readily available to managers.
